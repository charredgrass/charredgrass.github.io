Frozen Bukkits

Frozen Bukkits is an in-development plugin for Bukkit for Minecraft. It will include a variety of fun commands and spells that will allow players to get Elsa-like powers and Anna-like cravings for chocolate.

Want to suggest a feature, look at the source code, or contribute? Check out the source code on Github!
